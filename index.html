We are known as one of the leading manufacturers and exporters of CRGO Silicon steel products. We have been servicing the power and distribution transformer industry in India and abroad with world class CRGO steel products.

About Us

With a vast experience of over 30 years in the field of electrical transformers, we have established our core competencies in the design, development and manufacturing of various products in CRGO Steel conforming to global quality standards. We are known for manufacturing high quality transformer cores, toroidal cores, and other electrical stampings for our esteemed clients. We have two state of the art production facilities in Delhi and Roorkee with all in-house facilities for slitting, cutting, annealing and testing of transformer laminations and CRGO stampings. JMD Industries Pvt. Ltd. was established in 1988 as a manufacturer of CRGO Silicon steel cores for the emerging transformer industry of India.

With two units located in Roorkee, the manufacturing facilities are centrally positioned for supply of all kinds of CRGO Silicon steel products for transformers - power/distribution and special applications. Various reputed manufacturers of distribution and power transformers rely on JMD Industries for world-class products and an excellent quality of collaboration. With state of the art production facilities spread across a total area of 11,500 sq. ft., we have established our core competencies in the design, development and manufacturing of CRGO steel cores conforming to global standards of quality. At JMD Industries, supplying quality products at competitive pricing, with timely deliveries is our specialty.
